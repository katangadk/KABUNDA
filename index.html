01 / About

Who I Am

A cybersecurity student with a hacker's curiosity and a defender's mindset.

I'm Kabunda Katanga, a cybersecurity student at Zambai University College of Technology (ZUT), deeply committed to mastering the art and science of digital security.

My philosophy is simple: you cannot defend what you don't understand. That belief drives me into CTF arenas, cryptographic rabbit holes, and network labs — always chasing a deeper understanding of how systems work and how they break.

I'm building a foundation across offensive and defensive disciplines — from penetration testing and vulnerability analysis to digital forensics and incident response — with the goal of contributing meaningfully to Africa's growing cybersecurity landscape.
